Camp Rotary

Made with...


I set out to capture the fun paced environment of Camp Rotary, which meant getting involve with the kids. I shot all footage on a DSLR, GoPro, and Drone to get the right tone and energy. Footage was edited in Premiere Pro, with pop-ups made in AfterEffects, and made the soundtrack in Ableton Live.
Camp Rotary is a private, nonprofit, educational, independent, co-ed overnight camp for kids ages 7 - 15. The camp has a rich history dating back to 1921 when it was founded as a service project of the Lynn Rotary Club. Camp Rotary happens to have been a super positive influence on myself and I can't express how great it was to help such an awesome organization with this project. For more information go www.camprotary.org
