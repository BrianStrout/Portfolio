This promotional video for Ghost In The Machine's tattoo artist was shot and styled to show off a round robin styled display of the artist's favorite pieces from photos. I used motion graphics from After Effects, and shot footage to help make the presentation feel present, and dynamic. The soundtrack and sound effects were composed/edited in Ableton Live
Ghost in the Machine Tattoo is Boston's premier tattoo studio, boasting some of the finest talent in New England. Whether you're looking for large custom body work or a classic walk-in tattoo, we've got you covered. With over 75 years combined experience we are proficient in every style of tattooing.
